Between the years 1970 and 1976 and again 1984  “The Lord Chaitanya Festival” was held in Berkeley by disciples of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, the founder Acharya for the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (The “Hare Krishna” Movement). 

Who is Lord Chaitanya?

Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu is the Pioneer of a great social movement that began in India over 520 years ago and that has directly and indirectly influenced the subsequent course of religious and philosophical thinking not only in India but through out the world. That Sri Krishna Chaitanya’s influence has spread so far is due in large part to the efforts of His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, the present work’s translator and commentator and the founder and Acharya (spiritual master) of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu is thus a figure of great historical significance. However, our conventional method of historical analysis- that of

seeing  a man as a product of his times- fails here, for Lord Sri Krishna Chatanya is a personality who transcends the limited scope of historical settings. The Sri Chaitanya Charitamrta, by Srila Krishnadas Kaviraja Gosvami, is the principle work on the life and teachings of Sri Krishna Chaitanya Mahaprabhu. (more ..)

The Lord Chaitanya Spring Festival


Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu’s mission and philosophy was to alleviate the heavy burdens of Karmic activity that is present on Mother Earth; killing, deceit, envy, lust, anger, and greed are just a few. By example, Lord Chaitanya taught that by establishing an ideal of a common cause, as outlined in the Vedic texts such as the Srimad-Bhavatam, every living entity can co exist in great harmony. We employ you come to this festival with a mood of love and offering. Please bring your flowers and decorations. Come  dressed up! with enthusiasm as to celebrate the arrival of spring; attend with Lord Chaitanya’s revolutionary spirit, come with instruments, join the entourage, and part take in this historic event. This event will be saturated with insight, light, love and knowledge. Your presence will be more than appreciated, it will be adored by all! This event is wheel chair accessible and family oriented.


What To Expect At The Festival?


Lord Chaitanya Mahaphrabu’s method and message of peace was to resound mantras (hyms or songs in Glorification of God) in a musical presentation called Kirtan or Sankirtana (“san” meaning “complete” and “Kirtan” meaning “Glorification of God”). The parade will include not only Sankirtan, but great music, decorated floats, flags, banners, and colorful people dressed in extravagant attire. At the end of the parade you can experience the exhibits on the culture of Lord Chaitanya and His philosophy as well as Art and Gifts from Asia and great Vegetarian and Vegan items for free and for sale.
