Invitation To All !

We invite all communities to come together to this colorful parade and festival in the East Bay region of Northern California. This year the festival is on March 20th, which is The Earth Day Equinox. The Equinox is special because we contemplate the ways to pay our respects to our Mother Earth by relieving her great burdens. Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu’s mission and philosophy was to alleviate the heavy burdens of Karmic activity present on Mother Earth; killing, deceit, envy, lust, anger, and greed are just a few. By example, Lord Chaitanya taught that by establishing an ideal of “a common cause”, as outlined in Vedic texts such as the Srimad-Bhavatam, every living entity can co exist in great harmony. We employ you come with a mood of love and offering. Please bring your flowers and decorations. Come  dressed up! with enthusiasm; celebrate the arrival of spring; celebrate Earth Day!, attend with Lord Chaitanya’s revolutionary spirit. Come with instruments, join the entourage, and part take in this historic event. This event will be saturated with insight, light, love and knowledge. Your presence will be more than appreciated, it will be adored by all! This event is wheel chair accessible and family oriented.


What To Expect At The Festival?


Lord Chaitanya Mahaphrabu’s method and message of peace was to resound mantras (hyms or songs in Glorification of God) in a musical presentation called Kirtan or Sankirtana (“san” meaning “complete” and “Kirtan” meaning “Glorification of God”). The parade will include not only Sankirtan, but decorated floats, flags, banners, and people dressed in extravagant attire. At the end of the parade you can experience the exhibits on the culture of Lord Chaitanya and His philosophy as well as Art and Gifts from Asia and great Vegetarian and Vegan items for free and for sale.
